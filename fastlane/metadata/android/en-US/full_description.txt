Budget Brewer is a personal finance app focused on manually handling your finances. Compared to other apps, Budget Brewer takes a hands-on approach, making you feel more connected and in-tune with your finances. Automated apps try to take the "set it and forget it" approach, but your finances really aren't something you can deal with in the background. Life, however, can be chaotic, and finding the time to sit down and map everything out in a spreadsheet can be overwhelming. Budget Brewer handles the hard part, with minimal automation. All you have to do it enter your data and go. There's no bank account syncing, and you never even have to sign up. Creating an account is optional, but can be beneficial, as it offers data syncing to be able to switch between devices, as well as the option of being able to export your data. The greatest part of it all? It's completely free. No subscriptions, no paywalls, no ads either, all because I believe financial freedom shouldn't be limited to those who can "afford" it.
